About

My journey into genealogy began over 10 years ago through a DNA test. What started as simple curiosity quickly turned into a deep passion for uncovering family history.

Through researching records, archives, and historical documents, I was able to trace back generations and discover stories that had long been forgotten.

This journey didn’t just uncover names and dates — it enriched our family’s understanding of who we are and where we come from.

Today, I share what I’ve learned to help others explore their own ancestry and experience the same connection to their roots.

Genealogy is more than research — it’s a way to reconnect with your past.
